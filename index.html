Visions and Goals

Being a part of CSESoc this year has introduced me to new people and opportunities. I want to give back by enacting my visions and goals as an CSESoc Executive and Treasurer:

  • Drive initiatives and the society's vision with the knowledge of the financial position to maximise available resources
  • Develop an all-in-one platform for internal treasurer procedures such as reimbursements, project proposals, inventory and receipts
  • Play an active role in the event creation and sponsorship process to help directors achieve their visions and goals
  • Foster a supportive culture where all members are encouraged and have a platform to raise ideas as well as give feedback
